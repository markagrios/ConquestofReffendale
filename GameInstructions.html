General Concept

This is a turn based strategic empire game. Grow and expand your empire and use resources and gold to conquer your opponent.


Gameplay Mechanics

During a player's turn, he/she picks a certain action to preform. The second player then does the same thing. Once both players have made their choices


Goals

The player who has conquered the other person's castle wins. On the other hand, once 50 turns have been played and neither castle has been conquered, the person who has the most amount of territorry wins.


Starting Off

When the game starts off, each player gets the same number of points. With these points players have the option to bid a certain number. of their points without the knowlege of the other player. Whoever bids the most amount of points gets to start first and pick where he/she wants his/her castle to be. The player who lost the bid gets second pick of where he/she wants his/her castle to be and starts off second. With the remaining points, each player spends points


Aquiring Territory

Each players castle gives the player and eight-by-seven square of territorry around the castle. In order to aquire more territory, players need to put cities and towns in his/her territorry. Every turn, each player's territory grows by a certain amount. The more towns and cities a player has within his/her territory, the more the city will grow each turn.
